Go back

DoubleB is the main mainstream specialty coffee chain. Definitly try the author’s drinks, as well as the Coconut-Almond Flat White

They’re conveniently located on every somewhat popular metro station and serve consistently well-made drinks. The prices are higher than other specialty coffee shops, but it’s manageable.

Espresso Review

As usual, i must note that espresso flavor very much depends on the beans used, and they change from day-to-day

DoubleB serves a rare espresso for moscow- every blend is quite sour, fruity, and with a barely-noticeable aftertaste of cigarettes, as one of my friends noted. It is immaculately pressed. Eight out of ten stars.

To Try

  • Flat White
  • Espresso

Insider Tips

On weekdays from eight to eleven o’clock each morning, all black coffees cost only a hundred roubles, which, at the time of this writing, is only one and a half euros- less than Milan.
